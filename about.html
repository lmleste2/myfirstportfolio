Background

I am a junior web developer and graphic designer with skills in HTML, CSS, front-end development, graphic design software, video editing, motion graphics, and more. I am studying for my Bachelors degree in Media Technology at Arizona State University. Before starting classes at Arizona State University I was taking classes in film production at Austin Community College. I have lived in Austin for most of my life and I have a very strong passion for film. I am a very creative person who finds the most joy in my artistic work. I have always been interested in visual media and the film/tv industry and I hope I can use my design and web development skills to find a career and a place in this industry.
